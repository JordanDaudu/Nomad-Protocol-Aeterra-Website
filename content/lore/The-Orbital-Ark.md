# Orbital Ark

When Aeterra was deployed, a failsafe was constructed: An autonomous orbital archive known as the **Orbital Ark**.

While it can no longer control the corrupted system on the surface, it continues to execute its "Preservation Protocol" from geostationary orbit.

## Original Purpose

- Preserve biological data
- Store cultural and historical records
- Maintain uncontaminated system backups

When the surface collapsed, the Ark severed physical links and transitioned to passive orbit.

It remains functional.

It remains alone.

## Current Role

The Ark no longer controls the system.

It only remembers it.

The Ark supports active Nomad Units by beaming down **Archive Caches**. These are high-density data packets materialized into physical form. They contain the necessary resources to sustain a Master Node, including jury-rigged industrial parts, legacy blueprints, and integrity patches.
